We've been making delicious Pizza and Pasta for over 40 years

Tony's Pizza is a genuine family owned Italian restaurant, using premium ingredients and combining over 40 years of experience we bring you an unrivalled dining experience.

Not only do we serve pizza, but we also offer a variety of genuine Italian style Gelato. Take away, or dine in at our outdoor alfresco dining area. Come find us today!
